Character Certificate Process
  1. Visit the official State Police Citizen Portal (e.g., UP Police CCTNS, Delhi Police).
  2. Register an account using your Aadhar/Phone Number.
  3. Navigate to the "Character Certificate" section and fill out the form.
  4. Upload documents: Passport-size photo, Aadhar Card, Address Proof.
  5. Pay the requisite processing fee (usually ₹50-₹250).
  6. A physical police verification will happen at your address.
  7. Download the certificate from the portal once approved.
Driving License Process
  1. Visit the Parivahan Sewa website (parivahan.gov.in).
  2. Select "Online Services" > "Driving License Related Services".
  3. Select your state and apply for a "Learner's License".
  4. Fill the form, upload Age & Address proofs, and submit the Medical Certificate (Form 1A).
  5. Book a slot and clear the online/RTO computer test.
  6. After 30 days of getting the Learner's License, apply for a "Permanent Driving License".
  7. Book a driving test slot, pass the test at the RTO track, and the license will be dispatched via post.
Filing an e-FIR

E-FIRs are strictly for non-heinous cases like mobile theft, lost documents, or cyber fraud. For severe crimes, visit the station.

  1. Go to the state police website or National Cyber Crime portal (for cyber frauds).
  2. Login with mobile OTP.
  3. Select the incident category (e.g., Lost Article, Cyber Fraud).
  4. Enter the details of the incident (Date, Time, Description).
  5. Submit the form and download the digitally signed FIR copy instantly.
Arms/Gun License Process
  1. Apply under the Arms Act, 1959 via NDAL (National Database of Arms Licenses) or NDAL-ALIS portal.
  2. Submit Form A-1 detailing the genuine need for self-defense (e.g., imminent threat).
  3. Attach Medical Certificate, Proof of Age/Address, and Police Clearance.
  4. Undergo a thorough background check and interview by the DCP/DM.
  5. Complete the mandatory arms handling training course.
  6. If approved by the Licensing Authority, purchase the weapon from an authorized dealer within the stipulated time.
Voter ID Registration
  1. Visit the NVSP (National Voter's Service Portal) or download the Voter Helpline App.
  2. Select "Form 6" for registration of new voters.
  3. Fill in your personal details, family details, and address.
  4. Upload a recent passport-size photograph, Address Proof, and Age Proof (Aadhar/10th Marksheet).
  5. Submit the form to generate a Reference ID.
  6. A BLO (Booth Level Officer) will verify your residence. The EPIC will be delivered via Speed Post.
Passport Application Process
  1. Register on the Passport Seva Online Portal (passportindia.gov.in).
  2. Click "Apply for Fresh Passport/Re-issue of Passport" and fill out the online form.
  3. Pay the fee and book an appointment at the nearest Passport Seva Kendra (PSK).
  4. Visit the PSK with original documents (Aadhar, PAN, 10th Marksheet for ECNR) at the scheduled time.
  5. Provide biometrics and photographs at the center.
  6. Undergo Police Verification at your local station.
  7. Once cleared, the passport will be printed and dispatched.
